The problem every MDM tool claims to solve

The same supplier exists three times, across SAP and CRM, with drifted names, two phone formats — and two different VAT numbers. Everyone merges it. The question an auditor asks is how, and can you prove the answer twice.

What makes this one different

Not "explainable AI matching" — the incumbents ship that. The layer beneath it: determinism and provenance you can put in front of a regulator.

Deterministic to the hash

Run the whole pipeline twice — even feeding the rows in reversed order — and the mastered state fingerprints to the identical SHA-256. An audit story no LLM-based masterer can tell.

Cite, or refuse — never guess

Every golden value carries a derived_from edge to the exact source row. A field with two disputed values is withheld and queued, not averaged. Unknown entities are refused, not invented.

Tamper-evident lineage

Golden records append into a Merkle-chained store; decisions are signed into an HMAC ledger. A forged record or a doctored decision is caught.

Sovereign & training-free

Pure standard library. No model, no network, no vendor cloud. Runs where your data already lives — a real answer to POPIA residency.

Cite or refuse →

A closed query grammar: it cites with provenance, returns both sides of a conflict, computes over golden values, or refuses. No free text.

ingest→match→survive→golden record→steward→answer
